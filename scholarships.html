Scholarship Information

The Putnam County Gator Club (PCGC) proudly sponsors scholarships for students attending the University of Florida. Gator Club® scholarships are awarded based on academic merit, school involvement and community involvement. Students applying must be residents of Putnam County, Florida.

Typically, five scholarships in the amount of $1500 each are awarded annually, with the number of scholarships being determined by the number of applications submitted to and/or evaluated by the Scholarship Committee. Scholarships are unrestricted and may be used for items such as tuition, books, labs, rent or utilities.

The deadline for our 2019 Scholarship Application is April 18, 2019. All applications must be postmarked no later than the deadline.
